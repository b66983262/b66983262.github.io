The Fast & Performant Binary Neural Net Library

I have built my own binary neural net library and I want to share it with the world. It is a clean, fast, and performant solution for building next-generation AI models.

The Vision

The world of machine learning is moving towards efficiency and speed. Traditional neural networks are resource-intensive, making them unsuitable for mobile and IoT devices. My library was born from the need to address this challenge. By quantizing neurons and weights into a binary format, it can achieve remarkable performance gains with minimal impact on accuracy. This isn't just about faster computation; it's about making AI accessible and sustainable for everyone, everywhere. My vision is to be the go-to library for building lightweight, on-device AI.
